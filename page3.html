Technology has changed the way students learn. Online tools allow students to work faster and stay organised. Digital learning materials are easy to access and can be used at home or in college. Communication is also easier because students can email, join online classes and share files quickly. Technology supports both independent study and group work. However, students should use technology carefully and avoid distraction while studying.
